Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

IGT Network ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application operates, what information is accessed, and how it is handled in compliance with Google Play policies.

1. Information We Collect

IGT Network does NOT collect, store, transmit, or sell personal user data to external servers. All core functionality operates locally on your device.

2. App Inventory (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES) & Usage Access

To provide the core functionality of our firewall, the app requires the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission to retrieve a list of installed applications on your device, as well as Usage Access to monitor data consumption.

  • This allows you to view your installed apps and select which ones to block from accessing the internet.
  • Crucial Privacy Note: Your inventory of installed apps and data usage statistics are processed strictly locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than local firewall management.

3. VPN Service

IGT Network uses Android's VpnService API exclusively to act as a local firewall to control background internet access for the applications you select.

  • We do NOT monitor your browsing activity.
  • We do NOT inspect, decrypt, or modify user traffic.
  • We do NOT log or store network data.
  • We do NOT route traffic through external or remote servers.
  • All traffic filtering occurs 100% locally on your device.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob) & Advertising ID

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide personalized ads, the app requests the AD_ID permission (Advertising ID).

Google may collect certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers and Advertising ID
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This information is collected and processed directly by Google. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads at any time through your Android device settings. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Notifications Permission

On Android 13 and above, notification permission is requested to display persistent VPN status updates (required by Android to keep the firewall running in the background). Notifications are not used for marketing.

6. Data Storage & Device Management

App settings, such as your VPN status and your list of blocked applications, are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences. No cloud database is used. Additionally, the app provides local device management tools (such as clearing app cache). These tools operate entirely on your device and do not transmit data externally.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

8. Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. User Rights

  • Users may revoke permissions (such as Usage Access or Notifications) at any time via device settings.
  • Users may disable the VPN firewall functionality at any time.
  • Users may reset their Advertising ID in Google settings.
  • Uninstalling the app immediately removes all locally stored data and settings.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary to comply with legal or operational requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page.
