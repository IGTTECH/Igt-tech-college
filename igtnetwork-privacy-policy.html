Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

IGT Network ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application operates, what information is accessed, and how it is handled in compliance with Google Play policies.

1. Information We Collect

IGT Network does NOT collect, store, transmit, or sell personal user data to external servers.

2. Usage Access Permission

The app requests Usage Access permission solely to:

  • Detect installed applications
  • Allow users to select apps to block from background internet usage

This information is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

3. VPN Service

IGT Network uses Android's VPNService API exclusively to control background internet access for selected applications.

  • We do NOT monitor browsing activity
  • We do NOT inspect user traffic
  • We do NOT log or store network data
  • We do NOT route traffic through external servers
  • All filtering occurs locally on the device

The VPN functionality is strictly for local traffic control and does not function as a remote VPN service.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google may collect certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This information is collected and processed directly by Google according to their Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Notifications Permission

On Android 13 and above, notification permission is requested to display VPN status updates and service notifications. Notifications are not used for data collection or marketing.

6. Data Storage

App settings such as VPN status and blocked applications are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences. No cloud database is used.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

8. Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. User Rights

  • Users may revoke permissions at any time via device settings.
  • Users may disable VPN functionality at any time.
  • Users may reset advertising ID in Google settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary to comply with legal or operational requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page.
