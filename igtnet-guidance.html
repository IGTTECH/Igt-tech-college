How to Use IGT Network

Complete User Guide – VPN Protection & App Data Control

1. First Time Setup

Step 1: Open the IGT Network app.
Step 2: Allow Usage Access permission when prompted.
Step 3: Allow VPN permission when requested.

These permissions are required for the app to block selected applications from accessing background internet. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Starting VPN Protection

1. Tap Start VPN button.
2. Confirm Android VPN permission.
3. Status will change to Protected (Green).

When VPN is active, the app prevents selected applications from using background data.

3. Managing Apps

1. Tap Manage Apps.
2. Select apps you want to block from background internet access.
3. Save your selection.

Selected apps will no longer consume background data while VPN is active.

4. Unblocking Apps

1. Go to Unblock Apps.
2. Uncheck the apps you want to allow.
3. Changes apply immediately.

5. Stopping VPN

Tap Stop VPN to disable protection anytime.

6. Understanding Advertisements

IGT Network uses Google AdMob to display ads to support development. You may disable personalized ads in your Google account settings.

7. Permissions Explanation

  • Usage Access: Detect installed apps for blocking.
  • VPN Permission: Control background data locally.
  • Notification Permission: Show VPN status updates.

8. Important Notes

  • The app does NOT monitor browsing activity.
  • The app does NOT collect personal data.
  • No traffic is routed to external servers.
  • All filtering occurs locally on your device.
