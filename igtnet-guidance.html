2. Blocking Apps from Using the Internet

Stop background apps from draining your mobile data and battery:

1. On the main screen, tap START PROTECTION.
2. On the main screen, tap MANAGER APP.
3. Scroll through your installed apps and check the box next to any app you want to block but dont check box to app you need to use for network taht time.
4. Tap After selecting apps click BLOCK APPS button you will see notification showing blocked Apps.

3. Unblocking Apps

Need to give an app internet access again?

1. Tap MANAGER APP Then blue button UNBLOCK on the main screen.
2. You will see a list of currently blocked apps. check the ones you want to allow online.
3. After selection of app to unblock Tap UNBLOCKED SELECTED. The app will instantly regain internet access and see notfication saying that Selected apps unblocked.

4. Cleaning App Cache & Storage (New Feature!)

Free up space on your phone and make it run faster by clearing hidden junk files:

1. Tap the Clean Cache or Storage button.
2. The app will safely scan your device for unnecessary temporary files.
3. Confirm the cleanup to instantly free up memory on your device.

5. Stopping the Firewall

To turn off protection and allow all apps to access the internet normally, simply tap the STOP button on the main dashboard ,if you want to remove icon Click Clean Caches .

6. Understanding Advertisements

To keep IGT Network free for everyone, we use Google AdMob to display occasional advertisements.

  • Ads may appear when you save your blocked app list, or when you stop the App.
  • Simply tap the "X" in the corner of the ad to close it and continue using the app.
  • You can manage personalized ad preferences in your Android device's Google Settings.

7. Important Security Facts

  • No Tracking: We do NOT monitor your browsing activity or search history.
  • Local Only: The app acts as a local firewall. It does NOT route your traffic through remote servers.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information.
