How to Use the IGT Video Tools Suite

Welcome to the IGT Video Tools Suite. Compress, clean, and edit your audio/video files instantly. Powered by WebAssembly, all processing happens securely on your own device. 100% privacy and lightning-fast speeds.

📉 1. Compress Video

Reduces file sizes while maintaining excellent visual quality.

✨ 2. Video Denoise

Applies an advanced spatial filter to smooth out visual grain, static, and pixelation.

🎧 3. Audio Denoise

Remove distracting background hiss, hum, or wind noise from your recordings.

🎵 4. Video → Audio (Extract)

Easily convert video files into audio files.

🎬 5. Audio → Video (Merge)

Combine a separate audio track with a video file.

🔒 Privacy Guarantee: Everything is processed locally inside your web browser.
