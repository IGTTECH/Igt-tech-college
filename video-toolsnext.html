How to Use the IGT Video Tools Suite

Welcome to the IGT Video Tools Suite. Our free, browser-based media utilities allow you to compress, clean, and edit your audio and video files instantly. Because this tool is powered by WebAssembly, all processing happens securely on your own device. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring 100% privacy and lightning-fast speeds.

📉 1. Compress Video

Large video files can be difficult to share or upload. Our video compressor reduces file sizes while maintaining excellent visual quality.

✨ 2. Video Denoise

Did you shoot a video in low light? The Video Denoise tool applies an advanced spatial filter to smooth out visual grain, static, and pixelation.

🎧 3. Audio Denoise

Remove distracting background hiss, hum, or wind noise from your recordings. You can upload either an audio file (like an MP3) or a video file (like an MP4).

🎵 4. Video → Audio (Extract)

Easily convert video files into audio files. The tool will instantly strip away the video and provide you with a high-quality MP3 audio file.

🎬 5. Audio → Video (Merge)

Combine a separate audio track with a video file. This is perfect for adding background music to a silent clip, or replacing a video's original sound.

🔒 Privacy Guarantee: We value your security. Unlike other online converters, the IGT Video Tools Suite does not require you to upload your personal media to the cloud. Everything is processed locally inside your web browser.
