01. About IGT Docs Tools

IGT Docs Tools is a professional, offline-first productivity suite designed specifically for modern Android devices. We believe that your private records, books, notes, and professional papers belong solely to you.

Rather than transmitting your sensitive materials to a remote web server, IGT Docs Tools performs every file operation—including PDF rendering, format conversion, handwritten sketches, and Optical Character Recognition (OCR)—directly on your device. This guarantees maximum security, absolute privacy, and seamless operation even when completely disconnected from the internet.

02. Getting Started & Secure Imports

When you launch the app, you will be greeted by the central dashboard. All file management inside IGT Docs Tools takes place within a secure sandbox directory.

1

How to Import a Document

To load a document into the app, tap the "Import Doc" button on the primary dashboard. This opens your device's native file picker. Select any PDF, DOC, PPT, or Image file. The app will securely copy this file into its local sandboxed storage space and register it in the on-device database index.

2

File Management & Sorting

Imported documents will appear immediately in your "My Docs" list. You can view, rename, search, delete, or sort files by name, file size, or date of import using the quick-filter bar.

03. Advanced PDF Suite

Unlock powerful tools to manipulate, protect, sign, and modify PDF documents directly from the screen.

✍️ Draggable Digital Signatures

You can customize signature stamps for contracts and approvals:

  1. Tap "Sign PDF" inside the PDF Editor.
  2. Create your signature stamp: either draw directly on the scribble pad with high-precision digital ink, or type your name using beautiful cursive calligraphy fonts.
  3. Confirm your seal. It is generated as an overlay stamp.
  4. Interactive Drag & Drop: Long-press and drag the signature element across the document. Place it on the exact page and position you want, then pinch/stretch to scale its size.

🎨 Advanced Draw, Whiteout, & Undo/Redo

Customize, highlight, or redact information within seconds:

  • Draw Pen: Sketch on top of the document. You can customize brush thickness and pick from a full palette of eye-catching colors.
  • Whiteout Eraser: Redact sensitive parts. Applying the whiteout tool places an opaque white cover over the text, cleaning out confidential data underneath.
  • Undo / Redo / Clear: Made a mistake? Seamlessly step back or forward through your markings using the instant Undo & Redo actions, or wipe the whiteboard clean with the Clear button.

🔒 Passcodes & Password Locks

Seal high-value PDFs with military-grade offline encryption. Set a unique alphanumeric password key to lock any file. Unlocking requires entering the exact passkey.

🏷️ Custom Text & Image Watermarks

Brand your paperwork with official indicators. Enter a watermark word (e.g., "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT") or load a logo image, adjust the transparency overlay, and press apply.

✂️ PDF Joiner, Splitter, & Page Cropper

Need to split a chapter, trim borders, or merge multiple files into one master portfolio? Use the PDF Joiner to append documents in custom sequence, or launch the Page Cropper to adjust frame padding offline.

05. OCR Scanner & Text Recognition

Turn physical paper into live, copyable digital text with our lightning-fast, secure OCR scanning pipeline.

Step-by-Step Scanning Guide:

  1. Tap the "Scan to PDF / OCR" action card on the home tab.
  2. Position your camera. The app utilizes intelligent camera helpers to display a real-time frame guide. Press the Capture key.
  3. Adjust boundaries on the interactive cropping grid to select the document paper and exclude desk borders.
  4. Confirm to save. The scan will be saved as a high-contrast black-and-white or color document inside the sandbox.

Copying Structured OCR Output:

After scanning a document page, the app automatically analyzes text blocks offline. Tap "Extract OCR Text" to view the results. The system intelligently detects:

Headings & Sections Organized hierarchy
Bullet Lists Clean bullet point markers
Paragraphs & Columns Natural wrapping bounds

Copy the processed text to your clipboard with a single tap, or export it directly as a clean text file.

06. Encrypted Security Vault

For ultimate security, IGT Docs Tools provides an isolated, encrypted folder called the Security Vault. This folder is locked behind a primary app PIN code.

Configuring Your Vault Access

Tap on "Security Vault" on the home interface. If it is your first visit, you will be prompted to register a secure, multi-digit PIN. Your PIN is hashed and securely written directly into the private system partition.

Moving Documents In and Out

Inside your My Docs list, tap on any file's options icon and select "Move to Security Vault". The file is immediately relocated to the encrypted sector, shielding it from standard lists and accidental views. Relocating a file out of the vault is just as simple: authenticate your PIN and choose "Unlock from Vault".

08. Notes Whiteboard & Sketchpad

Capture your thoughts, plan architecture diagrams, draw sketches, or take handwritten lecture notes using the Notes Whiteboard tool.

Sketch Controls:

Launch the "Notes Whiteboard" screen. A large, sensitive drawing canvas will open. Use the bottom toolbar to adjust your ink color (Black, Blue, Green, Red) and set stroke dimensions (Fine Pen, Normal Pen, Thick Marker).

Canvas Operations:

Erase mistake marks by activating the "Eraser Tool". Alternatively, discard all whiteboard layers to start fresh by pressing the "Clear Canvas" button. The canvas is completely responsive, supporting smooth multitouch pan-and-pinch gesture scales.

Exporting Notes:

Once finished, tap "Save Sketch". You can export your doodle as a raw high-fidelity PNG image or compile it into a single-page PDF document, placing it instantly into your sandbox index.

10. User FAQ & Quick Tips

Q: Are my documents safe and private?

A: Yes, absolutely! IGT Docs Tools stores your imported materials inside a sandbox partition protected by Android security constraints. The app operates 100% offline and contains zero code that sends files to third-party databases.

Q: What happens if I lose my Security Vault PIN?

A: Because we prioritize privacy, we do not store your PIN on any server. If you lose your Security Vault PIN, you will not be able to access the secure vault. Keep your PIN memorized or safely written in a secure password manager.

Q: Why did my OCR scan fail on dark paper?

A: For optimal text recognition results, make sure the text contrast is clear and the scanning environment is well-lit. Avoid shadows across pages and hold your phone stable during capture.

Q: How do I export my finished documents?

A: Simply open the document, tap the "Export / Share" button in the options panel, and select your preferred method—whether it's sending it as an email attachment, saving to custom storage, or printing directly.
