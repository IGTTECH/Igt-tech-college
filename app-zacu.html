Kuramo porogaramu zacu zikomeye kuri Google Play Store

IGT Network

IGT Network ni porogaramu nziza yo gukemura ibibazo bya MBs cyangwa Wi-Fi isohoka vuba. Iyi porogaramu ishyiraho umutekano kuri telefone yawe kandi ihagarika porogaramu utari gukoresha ngo zitiba MBs mu ibanga.

Ibikomeye ikora:

  • Kurinda telefone na firewall
  • Guhagarika porogaramu gukoresha Data inyuma
  • Kugenzura Wi-Fi na MBs kugira ngo hirindwe ikoreshwa ry’umurengera
  • Kuzigama Data no guhagarika porogaramu zose zishaka kwiba Data yawe

Niba ushaka ko MBs cyangwa Wi-Fi yawe isohoka buhoro nubwo iri nkeya, iyi porogaramu izagufasha.

IGT PDF Editor

Iyi porogaramu itanga ibikoresho bikomeye byo guhindura no gucunga inyandiko za PDF ukoresheje Smart Phone yawe ku buntu.

Ibikubiye muri porogaramu:

  • PDF Reader
  • Gushushanya kuri PDF
  • Kongeramo inyandiko & guhindura
  • Whiteout, WIPE
  • Gusiba
  • Kuzimenyekanisha & gushyira umukono
  • Kongeramo amashusho
  • Kuzigama nka image cyangwa PDF

Hindura inyandiko za PDF vuba kandi mu mutekano kuri telefone yawe. Shushanya, shyiraho umukono, shyira mu ibara, wohereze byoroshye — byose utohereza inyandiko zawe kuri internet.

IGT QR & Barcode Scanner

IGT QR & Barcode Scanner ni porogaramu y’ingenzi ku bantu bakeneye gusoma inyandiko ziri muri Barcode cyangwa QR Code ndetse no kumenya ijambo ry’ibanga rya Wi-Fi.

Icyo itandukaniye n’izindi porogaramu ni uko ibyo usoma bibikwa kuri telefone yawe aho kujya kuri database y’abafite porogaramu, bityo bakakoresha amakuru yawe, kandi hari n’akamenyetso ko gusiba ayo makuru igihe ushaka.

IGTT Camera

IGTT Camera ni porogaramu nziza yo gufata amafoto na videwo, kandi niba ukunda gufata videwo ndende iyi porogaramu izagufasha kuko igabanya umwanya videwo ifata muri telefone yawe.

Iyi porogaramu izarinda ko ububiko bwa telefone yawe buzuzura vuba.
