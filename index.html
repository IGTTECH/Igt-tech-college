Welcome to IGT & Tech College

IGT & Tech College is a trusted source of practical technology training designed to help students and learners develop in-demand digital skills. Our mission is to empower individuals across East Africa and beyond with education that prepares them for real-world jobs and opportunities in technology.

We offer a variety of courses including:

  • Mobile phone repair (software and hardware)
  • Computer hardware and software fundamentals
  • Website development and design
  • Digital literacy and productivity tools

Our training is structured for all levels — from beginners to advanced learners. You will find step-by-step lessons, practical examples, and support to help you succeed. Our content is available in English, Swahili, and Ikinyarwanda to reach a wide audience across Burundi,Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda,DRC and more.

Whether you are looking to start a new career, improve your skills, or launch your own tech business, IGT & Tech College is here to guide you.

Explore our courses, read our guides, and contact us if you have questions. We are committed to your success and are here to support your learning journey.
