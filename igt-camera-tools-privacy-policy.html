Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application IGT Camera Tools ("Application"), built for mobile devices. The Application is a professional utility hub containing camera controls, a video recorder, audio utility, file managers, and comprehensive photo/video editing capabilities.

1. User Consent and Information We Collect

Your privacy is important to us. By downloading, installing, and using IGT Camera Tools, you consent to the processing of information described in this privacy policy.

Device Permissions Requested

To provide high-quality camera and editor features, the Application requires access to the following sensitive Android hardware and software services:

  • Camera Permission: Used solely to display the camera viewfinder preview, capture images, take high-resolution studio photos, and stream native direct feeds for local recording.
  • Microphone Permission: Used for audio capture when utilizing the video recorder to ensure videos are annotated with high-fidelity synchronized sound.
  • Storage / Media Permissions (Read/Write External Storage / Photos & Videos): Used to read saved image and video files, facilitate custom multi-track studio edits, and save newly engineered or compressed final output files directly to your device's local gallery.

2. Locally Processed Nature of Your Content

Offline-First Principle: All video recording, image capturing, multi-track audio engineering, file compressions, and visual edits are completed entirely **locally on your physical device**.

We do not collect, send, store, or transmit your captured user images, recorded audio streams, custom session templates, or media content and draft items to any remote external servers. Your media is entirely under your control.
