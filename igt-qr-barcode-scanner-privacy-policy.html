Privacy Policy for IGT QR & Barcode Scanner

Effective Date:25February 2026

IGT QR & Barcode Scanner (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Camera Access:

  • The app requires access to your device camera to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • No images or videos are stored; scanning is processed in real-time on your device.

b. Scan History:

  • The app stores scanned codes locally on your device in order to provide a scan history feature.
  • This history is private and not shared with anyone.

c. Phone Permission

  • The app may request permission to place phone calls (CALL_PHONE) when a scanned QR code contains a phone number.
  • This permission allows the app to initiate a call directly from within the app. The app does not access, read, or store call logs, call history, or contact information.

d. Device & App Information (Optional):

  • For app performance and crash analytics, the app may collect anonymous usage statistics.
  • This data does not personally identify you.

e. Ads Data:

  • We use Google AdMob for displaying ads, which may collect limited data such as device identifiers and IP addresses for personalized ads.
  • Please refer to Google AdMob Privacy Policy for more details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To scan QR codes and barcodes accurately.
  • To save scan history locally for your convenience.
  • To display ads and maintain app functionality.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Scan data is never shared with third parties.
  • Ads data may be processed by Google AdMob as described in their privacy policy.
  • We do not sell or trade your personal data.

4. Your Choices

  • You can delete your scan history at any time from the History screen.
  • You can disable personalized ads through your device or Google account settings.
  • You can revoke camera access anytime through your device settings, which will disable scanning functionality.

5. Security

  • Your scan history and any data collected are stored securely on your device.
  • We follow standard practices to protect against unauthorized access.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy to comply with legal requirements or improve transparency.
  • The latest version will always be available in the app and on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
