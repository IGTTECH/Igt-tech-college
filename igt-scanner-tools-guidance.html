Software Guidance & Features

Complete step-by-step guide to mastering the professional IGT QR & Barcode Scanner

1

Launch & Setup

Open the app on your Android device. On the first run, the app will request Camera Permission. Allow this permission as it is essential for scanning codes directly through your device lens.

2

Scanning a Code

Point your camera towards any QR code or Barcode. Ensure the code is within the viewfinder. The app will automatically detect and scan the code. If you are in a dark environment, you can toggle the Flashlight in the top-left corner of the scanner screen.

3

Viewing Results

After a successful scan, the result will appear. You can see the content type (URL, Text, Contact, etc.). Use the action buttons to Copy to Clipboard or Open Link. If the code contains Wi-Fi credentials, you can connect directly from the result screen.

4

History & Organization

Every scan is saved to your History. Swipe right to access the History screen. You can view past scans, tap on any entry to see details again, or delete individual items. To clear everything, go to Settings and use "Clear All History".

5

Customizing Your Experience

Navigate to the Settings Screen to tailor the app. Enable Dark Mode for better night usage, toggle Sound Beep and Vibration feedback, or enable Auto-copy to Clipboard for faster workflows.

6

Enhanced Security

Protect your scan history by setting a History PIN. Once enabled, the app will require a 4-digit PIN every time you try to access your scanned entries, ensuring your data remains private.

Transparency: App Permissions

We believe in complete transparency. Our app only requests permissions that are vital for its functionality.

Camera

Essential for scanning QR codes and barcodes in real-time. No photos are saved to your gallery without your action.

Flashlight

Allows the app to use your device's LED to illuminate codes in low-light conditions.

Vibration

Used solely to provide a small haptic response when a code is successfully identified.

Internet

Required to open links found in QR codes and to connect with the IGT ecosystem for updates.
