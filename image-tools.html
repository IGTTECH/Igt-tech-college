Welcome to IGT Image Tools PRO

Your all-in-one, secure, client-side image editing solution. Read below to master all the powerful features available to you.

1. Upload & Start

Begin by dragging and dropping your image into the dashed box below, or simply click it to browse your device. All processing happens securely on your device.

2. Precision Resizing

Need a specific size? Use the Resize Settings in the right sidebar. Enter your desired width or height. Keep 'Lock Aspect Ratio' checked to prevent your image from stretching.

3. Freehand Drawing

Select a color from the color picker below the image, then click the 'Draw' button. You can use your mouse on a PC or your finger on a mobile device to draw smooth, continuous lines.

4. Click-to-Place Text

Pick a color, click 'Add Text', and type your message. The button will change to 'Tap to Place'. Simply click or tap anywhere on your image to drop the text exactly where you want it.

5. Adjustments & Filters

Enhance your photo using the Brightness, Contrast, and Saturation sliders in the sidebar. You can also apply instant creative filters like Grayscale, Sepia, or Invert with a single click.

6. High-Quality Export

Once your masterpiece is ready, go to Export Settings. Choose your preferred format (PNG, JPG, or WEBP), adjust the quality slider to manage file size, and click Export to download.

How to use

  • Resize: Enter new dimensions. Keep 'Lock Aspect Ratio' checked to prevent image distortion.
  • Draw: Pick a color, click 'Draw', and use your mouse or finger to draw on the image.
  • Add Text: Pick a color, click 'Add Text', type your words, then tap anywhere on the image to place it.
  • Adjust & Filter: Use sliders to change brightness/contrast, or apply quick filters like Grayscale.
  • Undo/Redo: Made a mistake? Use the Undo button to go back a step.
