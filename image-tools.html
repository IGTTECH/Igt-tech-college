How to use

  • Resize: Enter new dimensions. Keep 'Lock Aspect Ratio' checked to prevent image distortion.
  • Draw: Pick a color from the color box, click 'Draw', and use your mouse or finger to draw on the image.
  • Add Text: Pick a color, click 'Add Text', type your words, then tap anywhere on the image to place it.
  • Adjust & Filter: Use sliders to change brightness/contrast, or apply quick filters like Grayscale.
  • Undo/Redo: Made a mistake? Use the Undo button to go back a step.
