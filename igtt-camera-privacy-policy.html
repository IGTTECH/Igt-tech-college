IGT Camera (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Our app does not require you to create an account. We do not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

1.2 Device Permissions (Camera & Audio)

To function as a camera application, we require access to:

  • Camera: Used solely to capture photos and videos at your request.
  • Microphone: Used solely to record audio when capturing videos.
  • Storage: Used to save your captured photos and videos to your device.

This data is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, livestream, or store your camera feed or audio on our servers.

1.3 Photos and Videos

  • Photos and videos you capture remain on your device.
  • We do not upload, store, or transmit your media content to our servers.
  • All processing is performed locally on your device.

1.4 Advertising (AdMob & Rewarded Ads)

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Rewarded Ads: You may choose to watch video advertisements to support the app. This is voluntary (opt-in).

AdMob may collect and use:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Cookies and usage data
  • App interaction data

This data is used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads and to prevent fraud.

For more information, see:
Google Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide camera and video recording features
  • Save captured media to your device gallery
  • Show advertisements (AdMob)
  • Improve app stability and performance

We do not sell personal data.

3. Data Storage

  • Photos and videos are stored locally on your device.
  • No cloud storage is used by the app itself.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information except:

  • With Google AdMob for advertising purposes
  • If required by law

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request data access
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of personalized ads

You can manage ad preferences via your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app listing or website.
