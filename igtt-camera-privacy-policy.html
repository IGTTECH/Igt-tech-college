IGTT Camera – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 7, 2026

IGTT Camera (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

Our app may access the following information:

  • Camera & Microphone: Required to take photos, record videos, and capture audio.
  • Device Storage: Required to save photos and videos to your device.

Note: We do not collect personal data such as name, email, or location unless you explicitly provide it.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Enable photo and video capture
  • Save media files to your device

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Photos and videos are stored locally on your device.
  • We apply reasonable security measures, but absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

4. User Rights

You can:

  • Deny camera or storage permissions (app functionality will be limited).
  • Delete photos or videos directly from your device.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads via device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services

Email: info.igttech@gmail.com
Website: https://igttechcollege.com
