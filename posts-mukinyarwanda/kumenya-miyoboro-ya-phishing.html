Uko Wamenya Imiyoboro ya Phishing kandi Ukaguma Ufite Umutekano

Phishing ni ubwoko bw’uburiganya bwo kuri internet aho abajura bagerageza gukurura abantu ngo batangaze amakuru yabo y’ibanga nk’amapassword, nimero za karita cyangwa andi makuru yihariye. Akenshi ibi bikorwa binyura mu butumwa bwa email, SMS, cyangwa imiyoboro ya social media.

Muri 2026, phishing irakomeye cyane kubera gukoresha cyane internet hamwe n’ubuhanga bugezweho bw’ubwenge bw’ubukorano (AI). Intego y’iyi nyandiko ni ukwigisha abantu kumenya imiyoboro ya phishing no kwirinda ibitero byo kuri internet.

1. Menya Uko Imiyoboro ya Phishing Igaragara

Imiyoboro ya phishing ishobora kugaragara nk’iyemewe ku buryo bwa mbere. Ariko, utuntu duto dushobora kugaragaza ukuri kwayo.

  • URLs z’ikinyoma: Reba inyuguti zitarimo gukosorwa cyangwa utuntu twiyongereye, urugero amaz0n.com aho kuba amazon.com.
  • Imiyoboro migufi idasanzwe: Abajura bakunze gukoresha URL shorteners kugirango bihishe aho inyandiko nyakuri ijya.
  • Imiyoboro idahuye: Shyira cursor hejuru y’iyo link urebe URL nyakuri mbere yo gukanda.

2. Reba Ibimenyetso Bishyashya mu Butumwa cyangwa Emails

Ubutumwa bwa phishing akenshi buba burimo ibimenyetso byerekana ko hari ikibazo.

  • Gusaba bitunguranye: Ubutumwa bwihutirwa busaba kwinjira cyangwa guhindura password.
  • Kwifuriza muri rusange: Ubutumwa bukoresha “Dear Customer” aho gukoresha izina ryawe nyakuri.
  • Amakosa y’imyandikire: Ibigo byemewe bisanzwe byohereza ubutumwa bugezweho kandi budafite amakosa.

3. Koresha Ibikoresho byo Kureba Umutekano w’Imiyoboro

Ikoranabuhanga rishobora kugufasha kwirinda phishing niba rikoreshejwe neza.

  • Ibikoresho bya browser: Browsers za none zimenyesha abakoresha imbuga zidafite umutekano.
  • Online link scanners: Ibikoresho nka VirusTotal bishobora kureba niba URL ari mbi.
  • Email security filters: Abatanga email benshi bamenya kandi bagakumira imiyoboro ya phishing.

4. Wirinde Phishing

Kwirinda ni uburyo bwiza bwo gukumira ibitero bya phishing.

  • Shyiraho two-factor authentication: Ongera umutekano n’iyo password yaba yaribwa.
  • Gumana software ivuguruye: Updates zikemura uduce tw’umutekano abajura bashobora gukoresha.
  • Wige buri gihe: Menya uburyo bushya bwa phishing kuko abajura bahora bahindura uburyo bwabo.

5. Icyo Ukora Niba Wakanze Link ya Phishing

Niba wakanze link ya phishing, kora vuba kugirango ugabanye ingaruka.

  • Ntukinjize amakuru kandi fungura ipaji ako kanya.
  • Kora security scan yuzuye kuri mudasobwa cyangwa telefone yawe.
  • Hindura password z’ama accounts ashobora kuba yarangijwe.
  • Tangaza icyo gitero ku mutanga email cyangwa IT department.

Kumenya imiyoboro ya phishing no kugira imyitwarire y’umutekano ku rubuga bishobora kugabanya cyane ibyago byo kwibasirwa n’abajura. Ubumenyi n’ukwitonda nibyo bikomeye mu kubungabunga umutekano kuri internet muri 2026.
