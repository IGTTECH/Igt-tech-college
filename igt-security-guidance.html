How to Secure Your Android with IGT Security

This deep-dive guide explains every button, setting, and feature within the IGT Security ecosystem to ensure you are 100% protected.

CHAPTER 01

The Dashboard & Safety Status

Upon opening the app, you will see a large circle indicating your "Risk Level." If it is Red, your device has critical vulnerabilities. If it is Green, your defenses are active.

Quick Scan Button: Tap this for a 30-second rapid check of your most vulnerable settings.

Summary Cards: Swipe horizontally to see real-time CPU usage, Memory availability, and Privacy scores.

CHAPTER 02

App Scanner & Threat Removal

The App Scanner examines every installed application. It specifically looks for "Package Sideloading" (apps installed outside Play Store) and "Excessive Permissions."

  • Step 1

    Select "App Scanner" from the main menu.

  • Step 2

    Tap the "Search" Icon to begin deep analysis.

  • Step 3

    Review apps marked as 'UNSAFE'.

  • Step 4

    Click individual apps to see their Permission Risk Score.

CHAPTER 03

Advanced Tricks & Hidden Defense

Hidden App Detector

Spyware creators often remove the app icon from your launcher. This tool scans the Operating System for packages that exist but are invisible to you.

Uninstall Protection

Prevents thieves from deleting IGT Security to bypass your locks. Once enabled, you must provide a PIN to uninstall the app.

Automated Clipboard Cleaning

Hackers can read what you copy. This feature wipes your clipboard every 60 seconds to protect copied passwords or keys.

USB Juice-Jacking Blocker

Public charging ports can steal data. This guide shows you how to hardware-lock your USB port for "Charging Only."

CHAPTER 04

App Lock & File Encryption

Protect your Banking apps, Gallery, and WhatsApp using our hardened encryption engine.

How to use App Lock +

Go to "App Lock," set your master Pattern or Fingerprint. Select the apps you want to protect. From now on, those apps will require verification to open.

Adding Files to the Vault +

Open "Vault," select "Add Photos" or "Add Documents." Once added, these files are moved from your gallery to a secure, encrypted folder only accessible within IGT Security.

Chapter 05

Standard Settings Configuration

Optimize how the app communicates with you and its background behavior.

Sensitivity

Set 'High' for strict link scanning, or 'Medium' for a balance between speed and protection.

Notification Bar

Enable the "Persistent Guard" to see your security status directly from your lock screen without opening the app.
