What is IGT & TECH COLLEGE?

IGT & Tech College is International Groups of Technology and Tech College.

Our mission is to empower the future of the technology world-wide, including East Africa such as Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, DRC Congo, Rwanda, Burundi, and more countries.

In all our courses, we use three languages: English, Swahili, and Ikinyarwanda. Learning is a path to success.
