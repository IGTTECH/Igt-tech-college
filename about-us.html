    Empowering the Future of Technology Worldwide

    What is IGT & TECH COLLEGE?

    IGT & Tech College is International Groups of Technology and Tech College.

    Our mission is to empower the future of the technology world-wide, including East Africa such as Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, DRC Congo, Rwanda, Burundi, and more countries.

    In all our courses, we use three languages: English, Swahili, and Ikinyarwanda. Learning is a path to success.

    About IGT & Tech College

    IGT & Tech College is a technology education platform dedicated to empowering students and learners in East Africa and beyond. We offer practical technology training in areas such as mobile phone repairs (both software and hardware), computer skills, website development, and digital literacy.

    Our courses are designed to be accessible and inclusive. We provide materials and teaching in three languages — English, Swahili, and Ikinyarwanda — so learners from Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Congo, Burundi, and other regions can benefit in the language they understand best.

    We believe in hands-on learning and aim to bridge the gap between theory and real-world skills. Our mission is to help students build work-ready technology skills that can open doors to employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning.

    Founded by Seth Iradukunda, who has experience in technology training and digital education, IGT & Tech College is committed to providing trusted, practical, and easy-to-understand content. We strive to help each learner reach technology goals with confidence, whether beginner or advanced.

    If you want to learn more about our programs or enroll in a course, please visit our Courses page or contact us on WhatsApp.

    IGT & TECH COLLEGE ni iki mumagambo arambuye?

    IGT & Tech College ni International Groups of Technology and Tech College bisobanuye ngo: Amatsinda Mpuzamahanga y'Ikoranabuhanga n'ishuri rikuri rya tech.

    Inshingano zacu ni uguha imbaraga ejo hazaza h’ikoranabuhanga ku isi hose ndetse no muri Afurika y'Iburasirazuba nko muri Tanzaniya, Kenya, Uganda, DRC Congo, Rwanda, Burundi n'ibindi bihugu.

    Mu masomo yacu yose dukoresha indimi eshatu: Icyongereza, Igiswahiri na Inyarwanda. Kwiga ni inzira yo gutsinda.
