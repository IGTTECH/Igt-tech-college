Privacy Policy

Absolute transparency regarding your data, permissions, and security. Built to protect, not to track.

Google Play Compliant
1. Data Collection & Usage

IGT Security ("the App") is developed with a strict Zero-Data-Harvesting policy. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third-party brokers.

Security Analysis: We collect non-personal device information such as installed package names, battery temperature, and RAM usage exclusively for real-time security audits.

Package Scanning: The app utilizes the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission to analyze every installed piece of software on your device to cross-reference with our malware database locally.

2. Detailed Permission Disclosure

To provide professional security, IGT Security requires access to specific Android subsystems. Below is a full disclosure of why we request these permissions:

Accessibility Service

Phishing & Link Protection

We use Accessibility Services to scan URLs typed or clicked in browsers. This allows us to block malicious phishing sites before they harvest your banking or login data. We do not capture user text input or record private chats.

Device Administrator

Uninstall Protection

This permission prevents unauthorized attempts to uninstall the security app, ensuring that thieves cannot bypass your locks or tracking features without your master PIN.

Camera Access

Intruder Capture

When the "Intruder Alert" feature is active, the front camera is used to take a photo after 3 failed unlock attempts. These photos are saved to your local device sandbox and are never transmitted to our servers.

Notification Access

Notification Guard

We monitor notifications to filter out spam and hide sensitive message contents from the lock screen, preventing 'shoulder surfing' and unauthorized reading of your OTP codes.

SMS Access (Receive/Send)

Remote Anti-Theft Commands

Required for our "Lost Device" feature. You can send an SMS from a trusted number to your phone to remotely lock it, trigger an alarm, or locate it via GPS coordinates. We do not use this to read personal SMS logs.

Background Location

Remote Recovery System

Necessary to provide the location of your device via the Anti-Theft remote command system. Access is required in the background so that coordinates can be retrieved even if the app UI is not open.

3. Data Sovereignty & Encryption

100% Local Processing: All files stored in the "Privacy Vault" are encrypted using AES-256 military-grade encryption and stay physically on your device's internal storage.

No Cloud Backdoors

If you forget your master Vault password, Seth or the IGT team cannot recover your files. We do not have access to your encryption keys as they are stored in the device's TEE (Trusted Execution Environment).

4. Data Deletion & Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety policies, you have complete control over your data deletion:

  • In-App Wipe: You can clear all security logs, intruder photos, and vault items within the Settings menu.
  • Total Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently erases all associated local data and vault contents.
  • User Account Deletion: Users who registered for our security courses can request account deletion by emailing our support team.
5. Third-Party Integrations

To support our free version and process payments, we integrate with:

Google Play Billing

Handles all financial transactions for Subscriptions/Premium. We never see your credit card details.

AdMob / Appodeal

Serves relevant ads based on Device Advertising IDs. You can reset these IDs in your Android settings.

6. Play Store Compliance

This application adheres to the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement. We provide prominent disclosure regarding sensitive data access and do not monetize user-specific private data. Our primary goal is the protection of the user ecosystem.
